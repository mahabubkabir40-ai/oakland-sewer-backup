Frequently Asked Questions

Is a sewer backup covered by standard homeowners insurance?
In most scenarios, yes—provided you have a 'Sewer Backup and Sump Pump Overflow' rider attached to your property policy. Our contracted partners document every single stage of the extraction process with digital photo logs and structural moisture mapping to make direct insurance billing as smooth as possible.
Why is raw sewage classified as a category 3 biohazard?
Category 3 water, commonly known as 'black water,' contains highly dangerous pathogens, human waste, viruses, and destructive chemical agents. Attempting to clear a sewage flood with standard household tools exposes your family to severe health risks. Professional containment, personal protective gear (PPE), and antimicrobial restoration agents are strictly required.
How long does the structural drying phase take?
Depending on how long the water was standing, a standard structural basement drying cycle takes between 3 to 5 days of continuous industrial dehumidification and moisture tracking.
