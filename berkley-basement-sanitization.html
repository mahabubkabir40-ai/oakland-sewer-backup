Frequently Asked Questions

Why is professional sanitization necessary after a flood?
Wastewater and storm floods introduce biological contaminants, sewage pathogens, and mold spores deep into concrete pores. Cleaning with standard bleach or soap does not kill these organisms deep inside masonry, whereas professional sanitization uses EPA-registered biocides to eliminate all biological hazards.
Are the sanitizing chemicals safe for my family and pets?
Yes. We use EPA-registered, hospital-grade antimicrobial solutions that are designed to kill pathogens safely. Once the sanitized surfaces are completely dry and the space is ventilated, the basement is perfectly safe for humans and pets.
How do you eliminate toxic sewage odors?
Rather than masking smells, we use professional thermal foggers and hydroxyl generators that neutralize odor molecules at a chemical level, permanently restoring a clean, odor-free environment.
